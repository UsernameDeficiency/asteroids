------------------------------USER MANUAL---------------------------------------
Avoid the asteroids to survive as long as possible. Use the power-ups to your
advantage. In multiplayer some Power-Ups will affect the other player in a 
bad way. Navigate the start menu with Up and Down and select with Return.

Keymap player 1:
Accelerate......Up
Turn left.......Left
Turn right......Right
Shoot...........L

Keymap player 2:
Accelerate......W
Turn left.......A
Turn right......D
Shoot...........1
